About Us

We believe that life is too short to spend time on parking. So we at Parking Hero strieve to provide all our customers and stakeholders with a hassle-free smart parking solution. Our mission is to fully automate the parking spaces and bring them on a single platform. Founded by a group of budding women enterpreneurs Anviksha Dixit, Lavannya Goyal, Medha Gulati and Avishi Goyal.Visioning to make your everday life easier and stress free.
